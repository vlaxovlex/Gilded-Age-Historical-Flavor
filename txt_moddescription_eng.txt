We proudly present our mod, “Gilded Era”, which adds a considerable amount of historical flavor for many nations, and also makes changes to the AI, modifying its behavior to be more competent and provide a more competitive experience.


[h1]Features:[/h1]
-About 90 new journal entries
-About 280 new events
-Changes and improvements to content from the base game
-Improved AI
-Beautiful borders (see screenshots)
-Historical wars, domestic and foreign policy (see details below)

[b]Content:[/b]
-The Carlist Wars
-War of the (Peruvian-Bolivian) Confederation
-The Great Game (separate from Sphere of Influence)
-More Accurate British Dominions (New Zealand)
-Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo
-Great Famine in Ireland
-Second French Empire (separate from Voice of the People)
-Revolutions of 1848
-The Perry Expedition to Japan
-Crimean War
-Second Opium War (A Third Opium War is also potentially possible)
-Historical unification of Italy and Germany
-Historical reworking of the American Civil War
-French intervention in Mexico
-Second Schleswig War
-Seven Week War
-Franco-Prussian War
-Second Pacific War
-Russian-Turkish War of 1877
-British Protectorate in Egypt
-Historical diplomacy between great powers
-Berlin Conference
-New claims for historical colonization
-Sino-Japanese War
(and much more!)
(currently content for the game is made before the 1890s, in the future it is planned to expand the time period to 1936)


[b]Compatibility and Performance:[/b]
-The mod has good compatibility with global mods, it is highly recommended to load below all other mods
-One year runs about 3 minutes in early game and about 3 minutes and 30 seconds in late game on my i7-13620H, overall performance is about 70% of base game
-Please report any bugs you find
-If the launcher writes you "parsing metadata failed" do not pay attention, it is a known error but it does not affect the game
